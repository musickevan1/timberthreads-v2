# Timber & Threads Website v2 — Client Overview

## What We're Building

A fresh, faster version of your retreat website designed specifically for visitors on slower rural Missouri connections. Same content, same warm feel — just smoother and more reliable.

---

## What's Different (In Plain Terms)

### 🚀 Faster Loading

| Before (v1) | After (v2) |
|-------------|------------|
| Site loads in pieces, spinner delays | Loads all at once, instantly |
| Sometimes looks broken while loading | Clean, smooth appearance |
| 150KB of behind-the-scenes code | Under 50KB — 3x lighter |

**What this means:** Visitors on slower connections (cell service, rural internet) will see your site immediately instead of waiting or giving up.

---

### 🖼️ Gallery Improvements

| Before (v1) | After (v2) |
|-------------|------------|
| Loading spinners before photos appear | Photos display immediately |
| Blank spaces while gallery loads | Smooth lightbox when clicking |

**What this means:** Your beautiful retreat photos show right away, making a better first impression.

---

### 📧 Reliable Contact Form

| Before (v1) | After (v2) |
|-------------|------------|
| Gmail-based, sometimes unreliable | Professional email service (Resend) |
| You couldn't reply directly to inquiries | Hit "Reply" and respond immediately |

**What this means:** Every inquiry reaches your inbox, and you can reply without copying email addresses.

---

### 🧹 Cleaner Foundation

| Before (v1) | After (v2) |
|-------------|------------|
| Old code mixed with new | Fresh, organized codebase |
| 53GB of unused video files | Clean, fast project |
| Error messages on some pages | Zero errors |

**What this means:** Future updates and changes will be faster and cheaper to make.
